\chapter{AB Quaternions}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An AB quaternion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} A + a_{2} B + a_{3} AB
\end{equation}
These follow from an elliptic Cayley-Dickson construct on the A binions.
