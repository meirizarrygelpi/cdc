\chapter{ABC Octonions}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An ABC octonion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} A + a_{2} B + a_{3} AB + a_{4} C + a_{5} AC + a_{6} BC + a_{7} ABC
\end{equation}
These follow from an elliptic Cayley-Dickson construct on the AB quaternions.
