\chapter{AW Quaternions}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An AW quaternion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} A + a_{2} W + a_{3} AW
\end{equation}
These follow from a parabolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the A binions.
