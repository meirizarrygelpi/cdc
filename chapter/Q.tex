\chapter{SWX Octonion}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An SWX octonion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} S + a_{2} W + a_{3} SW + a_{4} X + a_{5} SX + a_{6} WX + a_{7} SWX
\end{equation}
These follow from a parabolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the SW quaternions.
