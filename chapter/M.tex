\chapter{ABS Octonions}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An ABS octonion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} A + a_{2} B + a_{3} AB + a_{4} S + a_{5} AS + a_{6} BS + a_{7} ABS
\end{equation}
These follow from a hyperbolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the AB quaternions.
