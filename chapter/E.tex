\chapter{S Binions}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An S binion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} S
\end{equation}
These follow from a hyperbolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the reals.
